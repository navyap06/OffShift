Company Leave Policy - Terms & Conditions

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. General Leave Policy

All employees are entitled to leave as per the company's leave policy. Leave requests must be submitted in advance and approved by the reporting manager.

2. Types of Leave

  • Annual Leave: Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid leave per year.
  • Sick Leave: Employees can take up to 10 days of sick leave with proper medical documentation.
  • Casual Leave: Up to 5 days per year, subject to approval.
  • Maternity/Paternity Leave: Maternity leave is up to 90 days, and paternity leave is up to 15 days.
  • Unpaid Leave: Employees can apply for unpaid leave if their paid leave balance is exhausted.

3. Leave Application Process

Employees must submit leave requests through the company's Leave Management System. Requests should be made at least *7 days* in advance for annual leave and *24 hours* for sick leave (except in emergencies).

4. Leave Approval & Rejection

Leave approval is at the discretion of the manager and HR. If rejected, the employee will receive a reason for rejection and may reapply if necessary.

5. Leave Carry Forward & Encashment

Unused annual leave can be carried forward for up to *one year, but cannot exceed **30 days in total*. Leave encashment is available only upon resignation or retirement.

6. Unauthorized Leave

Unauthorized absences for more than *3 consecutive days* without notice may lead to disciplinary action, including deductions in salary or termination.
